Humidity Comparison
The latitude of a city, does not have a strong correlation of the humidity levels. There are instances of high humidity cities that are far away from the equator, and low humidity cities that are near the equator. Even though a city may have a temperature of 40 degree C by the equator, the humidity level could be low.
